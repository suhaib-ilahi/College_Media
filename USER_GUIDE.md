## â“ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Account & Login
**Q: I forgot my password. What should I do?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the email instructions.

**Q: Can I change my username?**
A: Yes, go to Settings â†’ Profile â†’ Edit Username. Note: Usernames can only be changed once every 30 days.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Go to Settings â†’ Account â†’ Delete Account. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

### Posts & Content
**Q: How do I upload multiple photos in one post?**
A: Currently, posts support one photo/video. Use Stories for multiple images or create separate posts.

**Q: Can I schedule posts for later?**
A: Post scheduling is not available yet, but it's planned for a future update.

**Q: How do I add hashtags to my posts?**
A: Type # followed by your keyword in the caption (e.g., #CollegeLife). Hashtags help others discover your content.

### Interactions
**Q: Why can't I message someone?**
A: The user may have messaging disabled or blocked you. Check your privacy settings and theirs.

**Q: How do I unfollow someone?**
A: Visit their profile and click "Following" to unfollow.

**Q: Can I see who viewed my story?**
A: Story view counts are public, but individual viewers are not shown for privacy reasons.

### Technical Issues
**Q: The app is slow. What can I do?**
A: Clear your browser cache, try a different browser, or check your internet connection.

**Q: I can't upload media. What's wrong?**
A: Check file size (max 10MB), format (JPG, PNG, MP4), and ensure you have storage space.

**Q: Notifications aren't working. How to fix?**
A: Enable browser notifications and check your device's notification settings.

## ğŸ”§ Troubleshooting

### Common Issues & Solutions

#### Login Problems
- **Can't log in**: Verify email and password. Try "Forgot Password"
- **Account locked**: Contact support if locked due to suspicious activity
- **Email not received**: Check spam folder and add support@collegemedia.com to contacts

#### Upload Issues
- **File too large**: Compress images or use smaller files (under 10MB)
- **Unsupported format**: Use JPG, PNG for images; MP4 for videos
- **Upload stuck**: Check internet connection and try again

#### Performance Issues
- **Slow loading**: Clear browser cache and cookies
- **App crashes**: Update your browser or try incognito mode
- **High data usage**: Enable data saver in Settings

#### Account Issues
- **Can't change profile picture**: Ensure file is under 5MB and correct format
- **Posts not appearing**: Check privacy settings and internet connection
- **Notifications missing**: Enable notifications in browser and device settings

### Browser Compatibility

- Edge
